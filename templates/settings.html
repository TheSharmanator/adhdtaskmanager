KIOSK SETUP SCRIPTS

Paste these into your terminal to auto-launch the browser in fullscreen on boot.

Windows (PowerShell)

$WshShell = New-Object -ComObject WScript.Shell
$Shortcut = $WshShell.CreateShortcut("$env:APPDATA\Microsoft\Windows\Start Menu\Programs\Startup\TaskBoard.lnk")
$Shortcut.TargetPath = "chrome.exe"
$Shortcut.Arguments = "--kiosk http://localhost:5000"
$Shortcut.Save()

Linux / Pi OS

echo "@chromium-browser --kiosk http://localhost:5000" >> ~/.config/lxsession/LXDE-pi/autostart

Warning: Kiosk mode takes over the screen. Alt+F4 to exit on Windows, Ctrl+Alt+T to open terminal on Linux.
